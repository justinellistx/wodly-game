Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

WODLY is a fitness party game. This page explains what information we collect, why, and your choices. We keep it minimal.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to sign up for updates.
  • Team/display name — the name you type when you join a game.
  • Gameplay data — your position, rolls, and cards during a match, so the game works and others can see the board.
  • Device storage — we use your browser's local storage only to remember your game settings, your session, and that you've seen the health disclaimer. We do not use third‑party advertising or cross‑site tracking.

Why we use it

To run the game, and — if you opted in — to email you occasionally about new boards and scheduled worldwide matches. We rely on your consent for marketing emails.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is stored with our hosting and database provider (Supabase). If we send email updates, your email address is processed by an email delivery provider for that purpose only. Sponsor logos shown in the game are provided by sponsors; we don't share your data with them.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime using the link in any email we send.
  • Access or delete your data — contact us and we'll remove your email and any associated records.

Children

WODLY is intended for general audiences. If you believe a child has provided us an email address, contact us and we'll delete it.

Contact

Questions or requests? Reach the WODLY team by replying to any WODLY email, or via the contact details published on our site.
