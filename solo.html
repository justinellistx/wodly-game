Before you play

WODLY is a fitness game that prompts physical exercise. Exercise carries risk of injury.

Consult a physician before starting any exercise program, especially if you have a medical condition, are pregnant, or have been inactive. Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort.

You play at your own risk. By continuing, you agree that you are responsible for exercising safely within your own ability, and that the creators of WODLY are not liable for any injury, loss, or damage arising from use of this game.
